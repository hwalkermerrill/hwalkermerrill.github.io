Reputation and Renown: Trailblazer's

This group is recognized as a collection of pioneers, frequently breaking new ground and pushing boundaries. They are considered visionaries, leaders, and innovators who courageously explore uncharted territories, inspire change, and create new paths for others to follow.

Loric's Animal Companion

The zebra is a distinctive member of the horse family, renowned for its striking black and white stripes that make it easily recognizable. Native to the grasslands and savannas, zebras are social animals often found in herds. Campaign Traits and Powers

All living things may form a connection with the natural world around them. This connection often takes on the form of an eternal and unchanging bond with an animal spirit which, when awakened, guides and protects them through their dreams, life, and afterlife.

Each campaign trait requires the player to pick an associated totem; this totem must match or be compatible with the totem of any totemic-archetypes and spirit companions (or bonds) the player may have or later take. Players with animal companions and familiars are encouraged to choose companions that match or are compatible with their totem, though it is not required unless they have the totem-bonded archetype or are a shamans spirit. Eidolons and other spirit summons (like phantoms) will also have aspects of these totems, though they do not need to match exactly. Many monks and hunters often choose to emulate their totems when selecting animal aspects and styles.

Properly awakening a creature's spirit totem requires undergoing a painful day-long ritual that includes tattooing their personal spirit totem prominently on their body. When awakened within a totem warrior or channeler, these manifest as additional bonuses gained while raging, while amongst creature with a totemic or animal power, such as totem transformation or animal aspect, these totems manifest as an alternate set of bonuses that can be selected. These bonuses only affect the creature with the awakened totem, not their companions, unless they have the totem-bonded archetype or otherwise have the same kind of totem. All other creatures instead gain a single daily use of totemic power, which they may activate as a move action, and lasts for 1 minute.

  • Activating a totem causes the creatures tattoo to glow, and grants a small number of abilities. This is considered a polymorph effect. While in the form of their totem creature, such as via a dream journey or beast shape, they gain their totem abilities constantly and may use their totem magic during their transformation.
    1. They become better able to communicate with animal species related to their totem, as though they had wild empathy (totem species only).
    2. They gain a +2 sacred bonus on skills listed under each totem. This bonus increases to +4 at 12th level.
    3. They gain an additional power, as outlined in the totemic magic section of each totem, with a caster level equal to their level.
